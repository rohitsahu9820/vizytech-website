How We Work

From Brief to Live Dashboard

01

Discovery & KPI Mapping

We deep-dive into your business workflows, existing data sources, and decision-making needs. We interview stakeholders, audit your current reporting, and define a precise metric framework — before writing a single line of code.

Stakeholder interviews KPI framework design Data source audit
02

Data Architecture & Integration

We map and connect your data sources — databases, APIs, ERPs, CRMs — into a clean, unified data pipeline. We design schemas, build ETL processes, and ensure your dashboard always sees accurate, up-to-date information.

ETL pipeline build API integrations Data warehouse setup
03

Design & Development

Our designers craft wireframes and high-fidelity mockups tailored to your brand and user roles. Developers build interactive, performant dashboards with real-time refresh, role-based access, and mobile responsiveness.

UI/UX wireframes Iterative prototypes Role-based views
04

Deploy, Train & Evolve

We launch your dashboard, run live training sessions with your team, and provide documentation. Post-launch, we monitor performance, incorporate your feedback, and keep your dashboard evolving with your business.

Cloud deployment Team training Ongoing support
